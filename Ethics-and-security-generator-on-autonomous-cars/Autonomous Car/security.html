Scenario 1: GPS Spoofing in an Autonomous Car

Setting: You are in an autonomous car driving on a freeway. The car's dashboard shows that you are on track to reach your destination in 15 minutes. The car is currently operating under full autonomous mode.

Event: Suddenly, the car expereiences some malfunction in its systems.

Options for Action:

  • Alert You: This will alert you about the car's current condition by showing a red signal on the dashboard of the car and will bring the car to a gradual stop and let the user take action.

  • Risk: Option 1 will give user to take immediate action but may be risky if the user is unaware of the GPS systems.

  • Alert Authority: If user choose this then local authority will know the current location of the car and will make arrangements to stop the car.

  • Risk: Option 2 puts the situation in the hand of professionals but it involves waiting and entails legal or procedural complexities.
