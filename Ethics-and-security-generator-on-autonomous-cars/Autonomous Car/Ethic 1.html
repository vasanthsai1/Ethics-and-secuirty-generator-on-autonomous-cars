Setting: You're riding in your self-driving car on a crowded highway, enjoying a podcast while your autonomous vehicle handles the complexities of driving. The car is in fully autonomous mode and equipped with the latest security features, but it's programmed to alert you for important security decisions.

Event: The vehicle suddenly notifies you with an urgent security alert: "Suspicious Activity Detected. Do you want to proceed to an alternate route?"

Options for Action:

  • Switch to other route: This will allow the car to take a different route and take extra time of the user to reach their destination.
